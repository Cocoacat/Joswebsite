ABOUT - BXXFT

BOXXFIT is a boutique members gym, appealing to those who want to train with purpose, get real results and improve their lifestyle.
They provide a fun, friendly environment, focused on achieving your personal fitness goals no matter what your experience or ability.
Committed to supporting your fitness journey, whether it be achieving fat loss, training for an event, wanting to look better or gain confidence, their professional staff will listen and be on hand to advise you along the way.
Also, offering personal training to give you a truly bespoke service and because of their size and adaptability they can also work with small groups of like minded people that have similar goals. If you want to train with friends, you can, with the help and guidance of a professional instructor at your disposal.
Boxing and Xfit functionality is at the heart of what they do. For those people who want to try something new these superb disciplines can be adapted for novices and professionals alike and can provide the basis for a variety of metabolic workouts, circuit training and individual skills and techniques to help develop your strength and fitness.
