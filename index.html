Welcome

BodyWork Therapies By Jo operates in the Yorkshire area and offers a range of treatments which are designed to treat the individual in an holistic way, treatments which include hot stone massage, deep tissue massage, indian head massage, quantum breath healing, these are a few of my treatments aimed at supporting people who want an alternative complementary and holistic way of addressing health issues. We offer packages which can be tailor made for any occassion, all in the comfort of your own home or office.

At BodyWork Therapies By Jo our aim is to give the highest quality treatments tailored to suit the needs of each individual client. Our holistic approach gives our clients a sense of well being for both the body and mind, our ethos is to offer the very best in customer service and excellence, and to deliver over and above the standard of service you would expect to receive in a treatment room environment.

If you are interested in the mobile service a fully trained, qualified and experienced therapists from BodyWork Therapies By Jo mobile service will come to your location with all the equipment they need to ensure you will have a perfect treatment, this means, no travelling time, no parking fees and no waiting around busy waiting rooms.

My business is based in the LS8 area and covers the surrounding areas around Leeds. Where possible, travel costs will be kept to a minimum and local areas within a 10 mile radius will be free, after that charges of 40p /mile will apply.

If you find it too distracting at home to relax and prefer to be away from your own home, we can provide this in the comfort of the highly trained therapists home. These rooms are fully equipped to recreate the full treatment room experience where you can relax and get away from home pressures and interruptions.

For first time clients there will be a 10min -15min consultation at the start of the treatment. These consultation are free, along with any advice on aftercare to ensure that you will have no adverse reactions whilst helping you to maintain the condition of your skin, eyes and feet in between appointments.

Contact me if you'd like to discuss any aspect of your treatment or to book an appointment

About

My name is Johanna and I am the owner of BodyWork Therapies By Jo. I am fully qualified as a Holistic Therapist and I am fully insured with The Federation of Holistic therapists (FHT).

I am also the face behind Not So Balmy, a natural hand made vegan skincare range. As well as being a fully trained Massage Therapist I am also a fully trained professional Holistic beauty therapist, Aromatherapist, and natural cosmetic formulator who formulates natural cosmetics from scratch. The seed for Not So Balmy was planted when I wanted to know what cosmetic manufacturers were putting into the skin care products I was using on my clients. So hense, Not So Balmy was born.

You can shop my hand made skincare range from this website or visit notsobalmy.co.uk to find out more about my skincare products.
